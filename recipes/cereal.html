Cheerios

Description

A beautiful, handmade bowl of cheerios from scratch. It takes just 1 ingredient and, like, 2 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cheerios

Steps

  1. Grab the cheerios from wherever you put them.
  2. Grab a bowl
  3. Pour them into the bowl.
  4. Grab the milk from the fridge or, if you're a psycho, from wherever else you keep it.
  5. Pour cheerios in. Guesstimate the amount.
  6. Pour milk until you see the whites of their eyes.
  7. Pick up the bowl.
  8. Throw it into the trash. You didn't grab Honey Nut Cheerios, effectively, confirming you're a psycho.
