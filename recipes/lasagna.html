Lasagna

Description

A beautiful, handmade lasagna from scratch. It takes just 3 ingredients and 13 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Lasagna noodles
  • 1 14oz can Tomato paste
  • 3 TBSP Salt

Steps

  1. Open the noodles.
  2. Slam them, as hard as possible, into a glass dish.
  3. Open the tomato paste.
  4. Take a lick, that ones for daddy.
  5. Dump the can onto the noodles.
  6. Add salt and mix together.
  7. Add additional salt for taste.
  8. Place into the oven at 350 degrees (celcius). Do NOT preheat the oven.
  9. Cook for 13 minutes.
  10. Call the fire department and ask them to bring a homemade lasagna.
